I find it hard to write things about myself. I always find myself stuck between sounding way too professional or way too casual thats when a colleague of mine said “Why does it matter”. So here goes nothing


I was born and raised in Houston, Texas where I’m proud to say I still reside. Since I was little I always loved to fiddle with any bit of technology I could get my hands on. Well somewhere between Middle and High School I somehow forgot about my love of all things tech (minus video games of course).


Flash forward a couple of years past High School (3 to be exact), I found myself on a path nowhere near anything tech related and I was miserable. Thats when I was introduced to the world of Programming and I found my childhood interest and desire to fiddle had once returned.


Since then I dropped everything and made the bold decision to pursue the tech field. Now I find myself indulging on hours of code and geeking out over the countless innovations that seem to pop up on my timelines every other day.
